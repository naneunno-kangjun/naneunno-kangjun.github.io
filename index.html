About

I study Trustworthy Large Language Models with a focus on reliability guarantees, risk control, and uncertainty quantification. My work designs conformal-inference–based methods for safe LLM use in real systems and builds auditable pipelines for RAG and embeddings.

Research

MACE — Multi-LLM Adaptive Conformal Ensembles

Risk-controlled retrieval with group-conditional calibration

  • Achieves target coverage while improving retention under domain shift
  • Designed for reliable RAG in high-stakes settings

UEC — Uncertainty-driven Embedding Convolution

Probabilistic embeddings for robust retrieval/classification

  • Incorporates embedding uncertainty to stabilize ranking
