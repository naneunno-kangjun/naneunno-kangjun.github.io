About

I am an Integrated Ph.D. student in the Department of Statistics & Data Science at Yonsei University since spring 2025, advised by Prof. Kyungwoo Song. I work on Trustworthy Large Language Models, focusing on reliability guarantees, risk control, and uncertainty quantification for safe deployment in real systems.

Research

Ongoing

  • Statistical hallucination detection for LLMs — conformal inference, and group-conditional calibration for high-stakes use.
  • Measuring & mitigating LLM bias — bias auditing protocols (group & counterfactual), mitigation via calibration, reweighting, and data augmentation.

Previous

  • Tabular data prediction with LLMs — language-based modeling for OOV (out-of-variable) generalization.
  • Technology commercialization (TC) recommender with a Korean LLM — tag-aware, multi-purpose recommendation pipeline using TC-BERT.
